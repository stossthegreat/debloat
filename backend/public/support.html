Common questions

  • Cancel subscription — iPhone Settings → your Apple ID → Subscriptions → Debloat OS.
  • Restore purchase — open the paywall in the app and tap Restore.
  • Scan won't complete — good even lighting, face the camera straight on, remove glasses.
  • Delete my data — scans live on your device; deleting the app removes them. For anything else, email us.
