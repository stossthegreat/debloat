Debloat OS — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 July 2026

What Debloat OS does

Debloat OS analyses photos of your face to estimate facial fluid retention ("bloat"), renders an AI preview of a de-bloated version of your face, grades meal photos for bloat-driving content (sodium etc.), and gives you a daily routine to reduce facial puffiness.

Photos you capture

  • Face scans are processed on your device (face geometry) and sent over HTTPS/TLS to our backend, which forwards them to OpenAI (GPT-4o Vision) for the written analysis and to Replicate (Google Nano Banana) for the AI render. Our backend does not permanently store your photos — they are processed in memory for the request.
  • Meal photos are sent the same way to OpenAI for grading and are not permanently stored by our backend.
  • Your scan photos and results are stored locally on your device so your Progress tab can show your evolution. Deleting the app deletes them.

What we collect

  • Analytics — anonymous usage events via Firebase Analytics (e.g. "scan completed", "checklist ticked"). No name, no email, no advertising ID, no photo ever goes to analytics.
  • Purchases — subscriptions are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat using an anonymous app-instance ID. We never see your payment details.
  • We do not sell or share personal data with data brokers. We do not use your photos to train AI models.

Third-party processors

OpenAI (analysis), Replicate (image render), Google Firebase (anonymous analytics), RevenueCat (subscription management), Apple App Store (payments). Each receives only what is needed for its function.

Not medical advice

Scores, estimates (including "ml trapped") and routines are cosmetic, informational estimates — not medical measurements, diagnosis or treatment. Consult a professional for persistent swelling or health concerns.

Your choices

  • Delete the app to remove all locally stored scans and results.
  • Manage or cancel your subscription in your Apple ID settings at any time.
  • Contact us for any privacy request: appsdevelop2025@gmail.com

Children

Debloat OS is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

We will update this page when our practices change; the date above reflects the latest revision.
